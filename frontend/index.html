Pheromone overlay colors
Yellow = food trail (ants returning with food).
Green = food smell (emitted by food piles).
Blue = home / nest beacon (emitted by nest).
Red = danger / repellent (dead ends, pesticide, stale trails — ants flee from red).
White = many trails overlap (busy crossroads near nest or food piles).
